Questions

Honest answers to the things people actually ask.

Is it really indistinguishable from the real app?
In normal use, yes. Your build starts from a real, published app and is the same app — same name, same icon, same behavior. The only difference is that it was installed privately onto your two phones instead of downloaded from the store. Open it and it plays or works exactly like the store version. The private chat feature is only available in your build and only appears with a deliberate secret gesture.
Will it show up as an untrusted app?
No. Because the disguise is a legitimate app that already exists in the store, there is no "untrusted developer" warning, no out-of-place icon, and no special setting to toggle on. In day-to-day use there's nothing odd to stumble on.
Do I need to jailbreak my phone?
No. There's no jailbreak, no rooting, and no risky modification of your phone. The pair is built and installed directly to your two specific devices.
How is this different from Signal or just deleting chats?
An encrypted messenger like Signal protects the contents of your messages — but the app still sits on your home screen looking like a messenger, and constantly deleting chats is exhausting. An always-empty messaging app is itself the suspicious thing. zzup hides the fact that a conversation exists at all, behind an app that looks completely ordinary.
Can someone tell it's not from the App Store?
Not from normal use. It matches a legitimate store listing exactly, so a casual glance, a borrowed phone, or a quick scroll through your apps only ever sees the ordinary app. A determined professional with forensic tools and physical access is a different situation — see Honest limits.
Is it on Android?
iOS only, for now. The private direct-install approach that keeps the disguise clean and warning-free is specific to how iPhones handle apps — and I'd rather do one platform properly than two poorly.
What exactly do I get for the price?
A matched pair: two custom apps, one built for each person, privately connected so they only talk to each other. On both phones they look and behave like an ordinary app. The chat between you is the hidden part.
How do I pay, and is it discreet?
A free 1-week trial first, then a flat $500 per year — one price, no tiers, no surprise upsells. Payment is handled simply and discreetly — you can pay anonymously if you prefer — and there's no company name to explain on a statement line.
